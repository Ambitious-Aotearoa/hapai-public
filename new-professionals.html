New Professionals

New Professionals can help professionals in the first ten years of their career find their feet.

New Professionals is a great way to kick-start your career in the public sector. Joining the network makes it easy for you to form relationships across the public sector and learn from people who are good at what they do.

Join us

Who is New Professionals for?

Nau mai, piki mai! Our network is for all kaimahi working in, or with, the New Zealand public service, who are in their first ten years working and would like to know more about how the public sector works.

Most public sector organisations are members of IPANZ. If your organisation is a member, you are eligible to sign up to attend New Professional events and offerings for free and attend our training events at a discounted rate.

What we do

We plan and deliver the New Professionals programme. This involves delivering events, webinars, training seminars, and producing thought leadership pieces and articles for the Public Sector Journal.
