Who is Hāpai Public for?

Hāpai Public membership is open to anyone interested or engaged in the Public Sector. This includes, but is not limited to, people from central and local government, Crown agencies, universities, not-for-profit organisations, consultancies and private sector firms that serve the Public Sector.

We offer individual and corporate memberships

No matter where you are in your career, there’s a membership option to suit. We offer individual and corporate memberships to professionals engaged or interested in the public services. Our New Professionals network helps members who are new to the sector. All memberships grant you access to Hāpai Public resources, networking opportunities and events.
