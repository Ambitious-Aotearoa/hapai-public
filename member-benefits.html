Benefits of membership

Hāpai Public members gain a sense of belonging by connecting with others, contributing to leading ideas and exploring the deeper concept of what it means to be a public servant.

Shape the future

Contribute to Aotearoa’s future government by actively advancing public sector thinking and practice. By participating in workshops, discussions and events, members help support a thriving public sector where knowledge is built, retained and shared by many members.

Find your purpose

Connect with a deeper purpose by exploring issues and ideas that fall outside your day-to-day job responsibilities. Join a community of people that recognise and celebrate contributions to the public sector through the Life Membership Awards.

Build your network

Build relationships with other public sector professionals across agencies and with the private and not-for-profit sectors. Through events and networking opportunities, you’ll get to know people outside your day-to-day circle.

Develop your community of practice something something

Develop your own community of practice by taking charge of your learning and development. Attend events that pique your interest or reach out to other professionals via our member directory to connect, share ideas and collaborate.

Grow your career

For professionals in the first ten years of their career, our New Professionals network provides a great base of resources and learning opportunities that can help deepen your understanding of the public sector and gain clarity over your career path.

Stay informed

Stay informed and up-to-date on best practice, topical issues and the changing public sector environment. We provide regular insights and a wide range of resources that tackle the latest issues facing the public sector.

Advance your capabilities

Take advantage of discounted rates for training and development. We offer regular courses, training and events that help advance member capabilities from deepening their understanding of the public sector to effective engagement with Māori. We also offer discounted rates for New Zealand’s premiere public sector conference.

Connect with leaders

Meet leaders from across the sector. Connect with people recognised for their contribution to the public sector through the Life Membership Awards or learn from other leaders’ career paths through events, such as the New Professionals 'Meet the Chiefs’ series.
