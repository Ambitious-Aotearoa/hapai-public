Event overview

The Aspiring Senior Leaders Masterclass is a series of workshops that brings together emerging and aspirant public sector leaders with experienced practitioners in a forum that enables powerful, practical learning from the practitioners’ compelling experience and hard-won wisdom.

The Masterclass examines important aspects of modern public sector management – topics which leaders moving into senior positions need to master, through in-depth engagement with guest speakers that develops attendees leadership abilities, competencies, capabilities and skills.

The Masterclass is held once a year in Wellington.

Who should attend

Attendees will be identified and nominated for attendance by Chief Executives of public services departments that are Hāpai Public members.
