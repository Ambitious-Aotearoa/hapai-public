Our identity

Our new brand is designed to unite Public Sector professionals in our shared mission of enhancing, celebrating and contributing to excellence across our sector.

Our name

In 2024, we embraced a new identity, transitioning from IPANZ (Institute of Public Administration New Zealand) to Hāpai Public | Institute of Public Professionals Aotearoa New Zealand.

The name ‘Hāpai Public’ combines Te Reo Māori and English to create a unique bilingual identity. As Hāpai Public, part of our role is to provoke thought. Through Māori-led kaupapa, a bilingual name was chosen to do just that. Hāpai was chosen to encourage exploration of Te Reo Māori while Public helps give context to the wider purpose of the brand.

Behind the name

‘Hāpai’ in Te Reo means to support, shoulder, lift up, elevate, or raise. This verb, combined with the English, ‘Public’ a reflection of both our sector and our members reflects our mission to strengthen and uplift public sector professionals while fostering inclusivity and recognition of New Zealand's cultural heritage.

Our logo

Our logo evokes the idea of wind moving upwards over mountains. Symbolising the uplifting of ideas and the bringing together of people from across the motu. Combined with the square shape and sharp lines, the logo resembles a flag, a symbol of identity and unity and signifying a place where public sector professionals can explore and strengthen their identity.

Our colours

Our colours bring a sense of precision, trust, energy, optimism and freshness to the brand identity. It is led by our primary colours of dark green, white and a soft orange. It is supported by a panel of modern pastel tones.

Our photography

Members are at the centre of our work. Hāpai Public’s photography is human, welcoming and authentic and seeks to reflect the increasing diversity of New Zealand’s public sector.

Our brand system

Our wider brand system incorporates the weaving of harakeke through our pattern design. This weaving symbolises a stronger, more unified sector and our commitment to bringing people from across our community together, fostering collaboration, and new ideas.
