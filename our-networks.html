Our networks

Networks are another way we help our community come together.

Join one of our New Professionals networks or search our directory for other networks within the sector. Local networks help members strengthen their connections across the sector. If you are a member of Hāpai Public New Professionals you can learn, share and connect with other local members through these networks.

Form a local network

Our networks provide opportunities for networking and connection in localised areas. Networks rely on local member participation. If you would like to establish a network in your area, please get in touch.
