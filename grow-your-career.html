Grow your career

There are many ways to grow your career in the public sector. Our range of resources can help you get where you need to go.

One of the best ways to grow your career in the Public Sector is to move across different roles and agencies. Pursuing new opportunities at a range of ministries can help you gain experience, broaden your capabilities and deepen your understanding of a range of areas.

Moving around the public sector gives you more perspective and exposes you to a wider range of complex assignments and tasks.

What better way to grow your career than by learning from others.

Networking events are helpful for meeting public sector professionals from across the sector. Hāpai Public networks are designed to help members connect with their peers and take control of their careers.

Secondments, projects and assignments allow public servants to apply specific skills and capabilities to a new job role for a fixed term. They are a great way to experience a new agency, contribute to meaningful work and access new development opportunities without leaving your existing employment.

Secondments are temporary assignments that usually take place within an agency or inter-agency to ensure a continuation of service. As a secondee, you remain an employee of your home agency while taking day-to-day directives from the host agency. Secondments are a great way to test your capabilities without moving from your current role.

Project teams are usually brought together to work on a specific goal that may require input from many different agencies or organisations. Taskforces and project teams often work together for a limited amount of time until they achieve the goal or objective.

Short-term assignments are a way for individuals to temporarily assume the responsibilities of another role, usually in their own agency. Examples include stepping into an acting management position or filling a vacancy until the role is filled.

Public sector professionals are drawn to public services for a reason — contributing to work that matters and makes a difference to the lives of New Zealanders.

Hāpai Public gives public sector professionals the ability to explore a deeper purpose beyond their job role or responsibilities. Through corporate membership, many employees of government ministries and crown entities are automatically members. Check whether your organisation is a member here.
