Course overview

Public Sector 101 is a comprehensive introduction to the public sector, delivered via online modules that members can work through in their own time at their own pace.

Public Sector 101 consists of seven modules, each with a video presentation delivered by highly experienced public sector practitioners, and a workbook including presentation content and additional resources for further learning.

Learn from an incredible range of experts who have first-hand experience in the sector.

Who should attend

This online learning resource is ideal for anyone new to the public sector, or those who work in NGOs, local government agencies or businesses and deal with the public sector.
